This mod aims to solve a simple problem, only having a copper shortsword at the start of the game to defend yourself, for playthroughs with other classes you are left to struggle until you can obtain a starting weapon from chests or crafting, well no more!

Introducing a starting weapon for each class, crafted with just 10 wood you'll be able to obtain whatever weapon you want as soon as you chop down your first tree.

Features
-4 new early game weapons and an extra when used with Calamity

-High quality sprites (Credit to Dandy6_ on discord)

-Fully revamped weapons with unique functions (Credit to Nakamurash on discord)
